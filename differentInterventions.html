Cognitive Interventions:


Mnemonic Learning

Mnemonic learning consists of attention based training and difference methods of improving overall memory performance through visual attention skills


Spaced Repetition

Constant repetition and priming through exposure and association.

Physical Interventions:


Physical Fitness

Leisure activities and general physical fitness


General lifestyle changes

Opportunities to socialize, adjustments to improve quality of life and dietary changes.
